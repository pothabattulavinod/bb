About Us

Hari E-Techno School & Junior College is a premier educational institution committed to shaping young minds and preparing them for a brighter future. We blend the best of modern educational techniques with traditional values to provide our students with a holistic learning experience. Our school is located in the serene and picturesque environment of Rajanagaram, East Godavari, Andhra Pradesh. We offer a comprehensive curriculum that includes CBSE standards for school education, alongside IIT Foundation courses starting from class 8, preparing students for competitive exams like JEE Mains, IIT Advanced, and EAPCET. Our college section includes IPE courses, providing a solid foundation for higher education. At Hari E-Techno, we believe that education is more than just academics. Our programs are designed to develop the intellectual, social, physical, and emotional well-being of students. Through innovative teaching methods, state-of-the-art facilities, and a nurturing environment, we aim to produce not only academically proficient students but also responsible citizens of tomorrow. Our mission is to provide a learning environment that fosters curiosity, critical thinking, and creativity, preparing students to meet the challenges of the 21st century. Join us and become a part of the legacy of excellence in education.

Academics

School Education (CBSE Curriculum)

Our primary program builds foundational skills in language, math, science, and social understanding with fun-based learning.

IIT Foundation (Starting from 8th Grade)

In addition to the regular academic program, we offer IIT Foundation Courses to help students develop the essential skills required for entrance exams like JEE Mains, IIT Advanced, and EAPCET. These courses are designed to help students build a strong foundation in Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry.

Junior College (IPE & Competitive Exam Preparation)

For students advancing to junior college, we provide a 2-year Intermediate Public Examination (IPE) curriculum, along with specialized coaching for EAPCET, JEE, and other competitive exams. Our focus is not only on academics but also on comprehensive training for future college admissions and career opportunities.

College Education (Undergraduate)

Our college section is geared towards preparing students for higher education, providing them with the necessary knowledge and skills to excel in engineering, medical, and other professional fields. We focus on instilling a scientific mindset, critical analysis, and practical problem-solving.
